07經典 WSB Quotes
WSB 原句與解讀

"Iran declined fake deal which means we are getting real deal done soon"

這是典型 WSB 反向邏輯：壞消息被重新包裝成更大好消息，重點不是協議真假，而是市場願不願意繼續升。

"Senior Iranian officials confirm plans to transfer enriched Uranus to Israel"

把 uranium 寫成 Uranus 後變成全日最病毒式笑位，反映社群已把「sources say」新聞當成可隨時戲仿的素材。

"ENVIRONMENTAL FEE, don't forget the iranians telling us it will be an ENVIRONMENTAL fee, not a toll"

這句捕捉了 environmental fee meme 的核心：市場不信外交語言，只覺得換名是為了掩飾實際成本。

"Friday - deal imminent / Saturday - deal finalized / Sunday - no rush / Monday - deal rejected. Rinse and repeat every week"

用一行總結過去多日的 headline cycle，也說明社群對官方節奏的信任已接近耗盡。

"Bers are correct but the market keeps going up? How fake is this market and when the bubble pops it's gonna be 1930s"

這是熊軍最痛的自白：就算方向判斷有理，資金流、被動買盤與市場結構都可以令價格長時間不回頭。
