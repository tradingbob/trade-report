WSB 語錄解讀

"Semi bulls will come into the casino on Monday from rainbow colored lambos"

這句濃縮了半導體好友的信仰式樂觀：他們不是只押反彈，而是押一次羞辱淡友的開市大升。

"Bers had 1 whole day and didn't close their positions… They never learn."

好友借韓股急升嘲笑淡友未平倉，典型 WSB 式提前慶祝，風險是把外圍反彈當成美股必然結果。

"using fake money that doesn’t exist, to buy memory that hasn’t been made, to plug into GPUs that haven’t been built yet… chasing a mathematically impossible profit"

這是對 AI／儲存產業循環融資的尖銳諷刺：需求、產能、融資與盈利假設互相支撐，但任何一環失速都會反噬。

"My favorite market logic: 'This would be an existential crisis for the market, so it won’t happen.' Wall Street is more like Circular street."

這句點出市場常見的反身性盲點：因為後果太大，所以大家假設不會發生，反而令風險被低估。

"Regard Burry buying puts means you have 1 year after that for whatever he thinks will happen to actually happen"

社群對 Burry 又敬又笑：承認他可能看對長線週期，但拒絕因此放棄短線 squeeze 與 call options。
