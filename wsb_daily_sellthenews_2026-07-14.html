WSB 金句與解讀

"We're literally watching machines fight"

散戶感覺自己不是在交易方向，而是在旁觀 algos 與 market makers 爭奪 0DTE 關鍵價位。

"Sex is cool but have your options ever gone up 130% in a day and you're excited even though you're still underwater on them?"

典型 WSB 心態：期權大升都只是減少虧損，但情緒上仍當成勝利。

"Only lost $10,000 today - not bad at all!!"

虧損自嘲掩蓋了真問題：沒有清楚停利、停損同單日風險上限。

"Was excited to trade on my day off and lost $750"

交易頻率不是 edge。PDT removal 令更多細戶入場，但沒有流程只會放大手癢成本。

"green into Thursday's 'at war' announcement... red open Monday into green again by 11am"

社群用陰謀劇本解釋市場節奏，反映對 headline risk 與政策時點高度不信任。

"Imagine holding options to expiry because of your conviction but it turns out you are just retarded"
