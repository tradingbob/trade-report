07經典 WSB Quotes
WSB 金句與解讀

"DEAL V3.68 final final v7 final.pdf has been leaked"

將地緣政治談判講成公司文件改版，精準諷刺同一份伊朗和談消息被反覆包裝成新催化。

"Pakistan, this is the seventh week in a row you've shown Peace With Iran in class"

社群已完全識穿 Pakistan mediator narrative 是重複使用的市場工具，笑點來自『又係你』的疲勞感。

"I think I'm actually mentally deficient for not cashing out my 1000% and then losing even more"

典型 WSB 自嘲：明知應該止賺，仍被貪婪拖到倒輸，反映短期期權交易的行為弱點。

"I used to think of the market as this precise instrument, pricing in everything with complex formulas, but now I realize it's just randomly moving on some rumors"

這不是單純抱怨，而是信念崩塌。社群由相信市場效率，轉向認為 headline 與算法才是短線主宰。

"algos from 2:50 to 3pm: literally sell everything; algos from 3pm onwards: literally buy every single stock"

用擬人化方式描述尾市 V 形反轉，核心意思是價格並非來自基本面，而是機械式追 headline 與流動性。
